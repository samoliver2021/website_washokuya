About Us (私たちに関しては)

Washokuya is a Japanese restaurant that has been open since 2020 and is now already open 3 stores across Bandung. Our goal is to satisfy our customers with flavourful, fresh, and high-quality food cooked by the chef who came across from Japan because customer satisfaction is our priority. All food in here is also Halal so can be eaten by all people. To make the better experience, we make our restaurant designed similar to that in Japan so that customers can feel the atmosphere like being in Japan.
